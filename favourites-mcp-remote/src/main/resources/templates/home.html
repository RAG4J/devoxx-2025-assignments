Favourites Manager

Welcome to the Favourites Manager! This application allows you to manage your favourite talks and speakers. You can add new favourite talks and view all your saved favourites with optional filtering by speaker name.

  • View Favourites - View all your favourite talks with optional filtering by speaker name.
  • Add Favourite - Add a new favourite talk to your collection.
Getting Started: Start by adding your first favourite talk, then you can view and filter through all your favourites.
